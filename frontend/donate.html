ℹ️ How Donations Work

1. Select an active campaign from the dropdown (loaded from blockchain)

2. Enter the amount in ETH you want to donate

3. Click "Donate via MetaMask" — MetaMask will ask you to confirm the transaction

4. After confirmation, your donation is recorded on-chain permanently

5. View your transaction on Sepolia Etherscan
