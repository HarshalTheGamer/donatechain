Why DonateChain?

🔒

Immutable Records

Every donation is permanently recorded on the Ethereum blockchain. No admin can alter or delete records.

🗳️

DAO Governance

Community token holders vote to approve fund releases. No single admin can unilaterally release funds.

📄

IPFS Documents

Campaign documents are stored on IPFS — tamper-proof, decentralized, and permanently accessible worldwide.

🔍

Full Transparency

Anyone can audit the entire donation history on Etherscan. Public blockchain = zero trust required.
